Fire Safety and Prevention

A smoke alarm is critical for detecting fires early and could prevent loss of life and damage of property, as it can alert people that a fire may occur or is happening at the moment giving enough time to evacuate the area. It is said that installing smoke detectors already reduced your chances of dying in a fire by 50%.
Fire extingushiers are important in reducing a fire or putting it completely, it is a fast response to a fire and a quick way of putting it out during the moment. A home should always have atleast one fire extingusher in the case of a fire, as it can help save lives.
Have a escape plan in the case of a fire, and make sure everyone in your home knows the plan and where to meet each other after safely evacuating the fire.
Always be attentive when cooking in the kitchen, check if you have turned off the stove, and never leave the kitchen premises when cooking keep flammable things far from the stove and always ensure that your appliances are working properly to reduce malfunctions.
